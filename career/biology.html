Medical Field

MBBS: Become a doctor by pursuing MBBS after completing a biology-focused science stream.

BDS: Specialize in oral health and dental surgery with BDS.

Nursing: A rewarding healthcare career, from registered nurses to nurse practitioners.

Pharmacy: BPharm leads to roles in drug development, sales, and pharmacy management.

Biotechnology and Research

Biotechnology: Work in pharmaceuticals, biofuels, and genetic engineering.

Genetics: Study genes in medicine, agriculture, and forensic science.

Biomedical Research: Focus on diseases, treatments, and healthcare innovations.

Environmental Sciences

Environmental Science: Work in conservation, climate change, and pollution control.

Marine Biology: Study ocean ecosystems and marine life.

Agriculture: Improve food production and sustainability.

Forensic Science

Forensic Science: Apply biology and chemistry to solve criminal cases.

Zoology and Veterinary Sciences

Zoology: Study animal behavior, genetics, and conservation.

Veterinary Sciences: Diagnose and treat animal diseases.
